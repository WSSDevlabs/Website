# WSS DevLabs Service Summary and Pricing Research

Date prepared: 2026-07-02

Purpose: summarize all services currently listed in the WSS DevLabs website source content and provide practical pricing research ranges for future pricing/package decisions.

Important note: this is research guidance, not final public pricing. Final prices should consider project scope, seniority, revisions, support level, timeline pressure, client risk, and whether WSS DevLabs wants to compete as a premium custom studio or an affordable SME provider.

## Recommended Package Structure

### 1. Web Launch Package

Recommended starting price: RM 3,000-RM 6,000

Best for: founders, SMEs, service businesses, simple company sites.

Include:

- Up to 5-8 pages
- Responsive custom design
- Basic SEO setup
- Contact form and WhatsApp link
- Deployment
- 30 days support

Avoid including:

- Custom CMS
- E-commerce
- Advanced animations
- Copywriting beyond light editing
- Ongoing maintenance

### 2. Growth Website Package

Recommended starting price: RM 8,000-RM 20,000+

Best for: companies that need stronger conversion, SEO structure, CMS, case studies, or content architecture.

Include:

- Custom information architecture
- CMS-backed content where needed
- Conversion-focused design
- Analytics setup
- Performance and accessibility QA
- 60 days support

### 3. Product / App MVP Package

Recommended starting price: RM 25,000-RM 60,000+

Best for: mobile app MVPs, web apps, dashboards, portals, client portals, booking systems.

Include:

- UX flows and clickable prototype
- Authentication and roles where needed
- API/backend integration
- Core app screens
- QA and staging
- Launch support

Note: the current "Build from RM 10,000" tier can remain, but should be framed as "from" for small modules or MVP discovery/prototype, not a full production mobile app.

### 4. Custom Systems / ERP Package

Recommended starting price: custom quote, usually RM 30,000+ for serious systems

Best for: internal dashboards, inventory, order management, reporting, ERP modules, workflow automation.

Include:

- Process mapping
- Data model design
- Role-based access
- Admin dashboard
- Migration plan
- Documentation and training
- Support/SLA proposal

### 5. AI Integration Package

Recommended starting price: RM 8,000-RM 25,000+ for small AI integrations; custom quote for RAG or multi-system AI agents

Best for: AI search, document Q&A, content generation, summarization, automated workflows.

Include:

- Feasibility assessment
- AI workflow architecture
- Prompt/model integration
- Guardrails and evaluation
- Monitoring/usage cost estimate

Avoid promising:

- "Custom ML" unless data, budget, evaluation, and maintenance are real
- Guaranteed accuracy without clear constraints
- Unlimited AI usage inside fixed maintenance fees

## Pricing Risks and Recommendations

1. Do not let "Build from RM 10,000" imply a full mobile app, ERP, or production AI platform.
2. Separate discovery/design from development for complex work. It protects both client and studio.
3. Keep ad spend, AI API/token usage, hosting, domains, app store accounts, and paid tools separate unless intentionally bundled.
4. Put clear revision limits on branding, UI/UX, photography, and media creation.
5. Use monthly retainers for anything that has recurring responsibility: maintenance, ads, SEO, AI monitoring, analytics, and content.
6. Use "from" pricing publicly, but quote fixed scope privately after discovery.
